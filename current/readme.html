Click here to go to the roleplay section

Currently you can create a new chat to roleplay in. In the chat creation site you can pick a name and two characters
that you wish to roleplay as. After you have successfully managed to create a chat, you will be taken to the existing
chats. You will be given a list of all the chats and you can choose the one that you wish to see. You will then be taken
to the actual chat where you can add things to the chat.

We don't have a majority of our website working yet. Especially our theme. But we were preoccupied with making our roleplay
feature run.

Bugs

If you don't fill out a certain aspect of the form, you aren't given much of an option as to where to go. You can of course
just click the back button.
